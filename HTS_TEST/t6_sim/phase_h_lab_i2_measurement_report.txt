Cliff notes (from matrices above):
- Lab H: first large decode loss at 1000 Hz (30 dB D=85 vs D=100 at 0-500 Hz).
- Lab H: 2000-3000 Hz essentially zero decodes at 30/20 dB (D=0); sync still passes.
- Lab I: D=100/100 through 4000 Hz at 30 and 20 dB; 10 dB shows D~45-55 (SNR / 4D soft bottleneck, not CFO sweep cliff).
- Lab I vs Lab H: PRE_SYM1 + V5a removes the ~1 kHz V5a-alias cliff; residual high-CFO behavior in this host lab is dominated by 10 dB tensor/soft limits rather than a new 4-4.5 kHz decode wall at 30 dB.
